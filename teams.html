Our Leadership

Subash Yammada

CEO, Vimtra Ventures | Serial Entrepreneur | Investor | Mentor | Philanthropist

Mr. Subash Yammada, is a dynamic serial entrepreneur and the CEO of Vimtra Ventures, a diversified global enterprise. With over 29 years of leadership experience, Subash has cultivated an expansive portfolio across Sports Franchises, Private Equity, Venture Capital, Real Estate Development, Technology Investments, and Data Center Operations, establishing himself as a global business leader and strategic advisor.

  • Executed and owned 60+ successful global M&A transactions, creating significant enterprise value.
  • Founded, incubated, and scaled multiple technology startups, many of which have spun off into successful independent ventures.
  • Established a reputation for fostering innovation-driven businesses.

A visionary developer of luxury real estate and golf resort communities, Subash is spearheading large-scale projects across North America and India. Signature projects include the Lafayette Hills Golf Community in Syracuse, New York, and the Dalton @Noda 275 unit Mid rise Multi family apartment complex near Uptown Charlotte in North Carolina.

Subash has served as a C-level executive and board advisor in multinational organizations across IT, infrastructure, banking, and real estate industries. As a mentor, he actively guides the next generation of entrepreneurs, sharing insights on innovation, resilience, and sustainable growth.

Subash strategically leads Vimtra Ventures’ growth in data center infrastructure to support the increasing demands of AI and cloud computing. He spearheads greenfield and brownfield projects and fosters strategic partnerships with global technology providers to deliver high-performance, sustainable facilities.

Thimmaji Rao Yammada

Managing Director, Vimtra Ventures | Serial Entrepreneur | Mentor | Leader

Mr. Thimmaji Rao Yammada Venkata, widely known as Rao Yammada, is the Managing Director and Managing Partner of Vimtra Ventures. With over 30 years of expertise in infrastructure, real estate, and industrial development across North America and India, he has been instrumental in shaping Vimtra into a global enterprise. A visionary leader, he has transformed his ideas into a portfolio of thriving organizations, driving consistent and sustainable growth for more than 26 years.

Under his leadership, Rao Yammada continues to drive high-impact ventures in India, including the Arka Grand and The Windgrove projects. His strategic focus on entrepreneurship and investment reflects his commitment to creating sustainable developments and long-term value.

He has advised on the restructuring and repositioning of diverse property types—ranging from industrial facilities, hotels, and residential communities to retail malls and mixed-use projects—across North America and Asia. In addition, he has supported secured and unsecured creditors, loan servicers, and acquirers in major debt restructurings.

Alongside Subash, Rao provides the operational expertise for Vimtra's digital ecosystem investments. He has proven experience in managing mission-critical facilities with Tier III and Tier IV standards for uptime and resilience, with capabilities in scalable design, edge data centers, and high-density deployments.

Surya Kotha

Technology Advisory

Surya heads up the technology advisory in Vimtra. With over 24 years of experience in the IT industry in a variety of senior management and leadership roles, Surya is actively involved with a number of start-ups as an investor, mentor, and advisor.

Vimtra Ventures is a premier advisory firm specializing in high-stakes corporate finance and strategic development across the globe.
